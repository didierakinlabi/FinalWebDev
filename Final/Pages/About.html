DIDIER AKINLABI OSIAS
ART BOOKS HOME
Didier Akinlabi Osias is an artist and engineering student from Baltimore, Maryland. A child of immigrant parents from different cultures, Didier uses photography to capture his multicultural experience, to inform his practice as an artist, and to serve as his primary inspiration. As a photographer Didier is most focused on portraiture and has produced two photo books. Most recently, Didier has turned his photos into block-style prints which he has used to create a zine.
